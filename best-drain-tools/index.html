Quick answer

The TubShroom Ultra does this job better than any other model we’ve recommended. It sits flush, doesn’t need tools, and you only have to clean it every week or two. Our verdict

No single drain tool works for every situation, but a two-pronged approach handles most homes. Start with the TubShroom Ultra in every shower to catch hair before it clogs. Pair it with a Zip-It snake under the sink for the occasional kitchen or bathroom slowdown. If you have a kitchen sink that sees heavy food waste, swap in the OXO Good Grips strainer instead. For a premium bathroom upgrade, the simplehuman Hair Catcher is a beautiful, functional alternative. Whichever you choose, you will spend less time dealing with slow drains and more time enjoying your home.
